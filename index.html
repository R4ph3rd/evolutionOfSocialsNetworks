Evolution of features of social networks

This is an attempt to visualize the evolution of features in the history of social networking, showing how many of them copy each of them leading to a standardization. We concentrate on the most important (by popularity) of them in 2020 because they are concentrate the most of usages in social networking, and influence the rest of the web while also influencing each other.
Features are classified in 13 differents categories :
for devevelopers, reactions, layout, image & video, payment, advertising, sharing, moderation, search engine, apps, feed & content.

Each category is defined by a specified color giving it to each node representing a feature in the timeline of each network. To have more informations about a specified feature, hover it. To see which are linked by a strong similarity, click on it, and discover if it has been improved many times or copied by others !
